Methodology

This study aggregates publicly available data on professional and semi-professional gaming tournaments. Prize money totals reflect base pools and any community crowdfunding contributions. Some games without clearly identifiable genres in the source data have been categorized manually.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which game has the largest esports prize pool?
Dota 2 holds the record for the largest overall esports prize pool, largely due to its annual crowd-funded tournament, The International.
How is esports prize money tracked?
Data is compiled from historical tournament payouts, official announcements, and community databases tracking tournament results globally.
What is the highest paying esports tournament?
The International (Dota 2) holds the record for highest single-tournament prize pool, peaking at over $40 million.
Who are the highest earning esports players?
The highest earning players primarily compete in Dota 2, having won multiple editions of The International. Players like N0tail have earned over $7 million.
Which genre has the most prize money?
MOBA (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena) and Battle Royale games currently account for the largest share of total esports prize money.
